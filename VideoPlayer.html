HTML 5 <video> tag supports video playback in the browser. The most cross-platform format is the MP4 video format, although other formats such as ogg, webm and avi are supported by numerous browsers.
To play either mp, ogg or webm video in this simple web page, then simply comment out the respective section, add path in the "src" (do not remove the "file:///" part to conform to the URI path scheme standard) attribute and Enjoy!!!!
